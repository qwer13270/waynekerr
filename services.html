Service and Repair

Wayne Kerr Electronics aims to provide comprehensive support to all our customers.

In the event of difficulty, or apparent circuit malfunction, it is advisable to contact the service department or your local sales engineer or agent for advice before attempting repairs.

For repairs and recalibration it is recommended that the complete instrument be returned to one of the following service centres.

When returning the instrument please ensure adequate care is taken with packing and arrange insurance cover against transit damage or loss. If possible re-use the original packaging.
