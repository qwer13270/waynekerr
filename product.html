Overview

5400 is a super machine that combines LCR meter with the Impedance Analyzer. The LCR meter is 3 to 5 times faster than the 3260, and it could measure up to 10 MHz. These measurements and analyzations are all done with the 5400.

Completely characterize components graphically.

The binning function allows component manufacturers to sort components in up to ten bins. Sorting is carried out either by absolute values or by percentage of values.

Component tests with up to 300 A DC bias current for operation of up to 500KHZ

Component tests up to 50A DC bias for operation of up to 3MHz

Fast automatic multi-frequency production testing of capacitors and inductors

Protection against charged capacitors
